The rustic and bold character of Arts & Crafts styling is portrayed in the authentic Craftsman details found in Artisan Loft Bedroom.

Features:
Notable design motifs include exposed tenon detail, inset drawers with square raised frames, slat lattice overlays, cove undertop moldings and arched base rails
Storage pieces include a Door Dresser topped by a Landscape Mirror with flat top crown and lattice panel insert
There is also a 5-Drawer Chest, a tall 3-Drawer Nightstand and an Entertainment Door Chest for media in the bedroom
Drawers have metal full extension side glides and removable felt inserts in top drawers
A Column Bed in queen or king size features large square posts with exposed tenons, oversized post caps and a lattice insert over the head and foot panels
The bed's sweeping concave curve crown rails complete its sturdy look
Artisan Loft is constructed of decorative quartered oak veneers over sustainable rubberwood solids with some engineered wood products
Its finish is a warm medium oak color that highlights its beautiful quarter cut wood grain
Cast metal drawer hardware is a clean lined bar pull finished in a dark rustic patina
