Standard Furniture Westchester Side Chair in Cherry [Set of 2]

Currently Not Available
Westchester Dining offers an updated formal setting with its grand traditional styling, beautiful carvings and veneer patterned surfaces.

Features:
Design details include splendid decorative ornaments such as shell and scroll carvings, beaded moldings, reeding, vase turned legs, oval overlay frames and deep ogee curves.
Westchester's functional Server has two felt lined drawers plus two hidden ogee drawers, a wine rack for bottles, and features focal point beaded oval frames on its doors.
A Rectangle Dining Table with two 18" leaves has vase turned legs with carvings and reeding, serpentine shaped top corners and reverse diamond patterned veneer tops.
Graceful Arm and Side Chairs have smoothly rounded shoulders with carved tops, an open oval center back splat and decorative vase turned legs. Cushioned slip seats are upholstered in dark brown PU with welt trim.
Westchester is constructed of sustainable rubberwood solids, cherry and ash burl veneers, and engineered wood products finished in a rich cherry stain with darker burnishing for depth. This group may contain some poly resin parts.
Ornate cast metal knobs and backplate hardware is finished in a dark aged bronze color.
Surfaces clean easily with a soft cloth.
