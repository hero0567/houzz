A.R.T. Furniture Belmar Open Nightstand

$407
* Price not verified
Limited Availability, Shipping Additional. Belmar conjures an atmosphere reminiscent of generations of summer afternoons spent at a family coastal cottage. A warm, casual environment is conveyed by heirloom like pieces, passed down from family to family through the ages. A beautifully unique, weathered hand painted finish is enhanced with nicks, rasping, worm holes and a sand-thru appearance. Several pieces are scaled down in size for a cosy fit in a summer cottage or condo, and the adapts wonderfully to a variety of lifestyles! A piece with a smaller footprint, but plenty of style, the open night stand has a serpentine shaped top, and octagonal posts for legs, ending in a bulb shaped foot. The finished bottom shelf follows the same scalloped profile as the top, and the entire drawer front echoes the signature scalloped shape. The top drawer is felt lined to protect jewelry.
