Enhance your living space with Crosley's impeccably-crafted Corner TV stand. This signature cabinet accommodates most 52"Flat Panel TVs, and is handsomely proportioned featuring character-rich details sure to impress. The hand rubbed, multi-step Classic Cherry finish with antique brass finish hardware is perfect for blending with the family of that is already part of your home.
