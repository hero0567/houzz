As advertised

By Tony on June 6, 2016Verified Purchase

Make no mistake: this is a great value for the price. It was packed carefully, arrived with no apparent blemishes, was easy to assemble (everything fit together), and seems solid. The cushion is upholstered in vinyl, as noted in the description. The only piece of "hardware" I'd quibble with is a chrome- or aluminum-plated trim ring where the column fits into the base. The description of the finish is technically correct, also, in describing the chair as "bentwood" and having a walnut finish. However, it should be noted that it does not appear to be a walnut veneer, or if it is actual walnut veneer, it is the thinnest veneer I have ever encountered. I have read reviews of this chair elsewhere which suggest the finish is very fragile and easily nicked and scratched. I will likely use a furniture paste wax to try to fortify it at least a little, and place it in such a way to make it less likely it will hit nearby objects or countertops. That said, for this price, I would be surprised to find veneer plywood of a quality of, say, a real Eames Lounge Chair. In short, it is solid, comfortable, attractive, and an excellent value for the price, as long as you clearly understand what you're getting.
