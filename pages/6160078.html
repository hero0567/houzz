Product Description

The 35.5" Ludwig Glass White Single Sink Modern Contemporary Bathroom Vanity Furniture with Glass Inlay Doors offers both contemporary and clean-lined sophistication. This modern bathroom vanity cabinet is made from solid oak and its glass countertop features an integrated glass sink. Tempered glass door inserts add character and beauty to each cabinet door, and a rich white finish adds dimension. Ideal for traditional or modern bathrooms.


Dimensions

*Vanity with top and sink: 35.5"W x 21.3"D x 33.9"H
