Love it!

By lisawalthall47 on January 18, 2017Verified Purchase

This is the cart I ended up buying after looking at many different carts on many sites. I wanted to build a custom cart to my specifications but that became size and cost prohibitive. The cart shipped quickly and was received within a couple days. The cart came in two very heavy boxes. The product is packaged very well. The granite top ships in it's own box and is surrounded by a wood crate and styrofoam. The small pieces (screws, dowels and such) are packaged with each type of item separated and labeled for easy identification. Each component of the cart is well identified. The instructions are spot on and not too difficult to follow, but definitely follow the directions! It was not hard to assemble but plan on spending a couple hours putting it together. It took my husband and I working together between 2 and 3 hours to get it completely assembled. The material is nice quality and the finished product is sturdy. It adds additional work space and storage to my kitchen. I'm very happy with this product.

Overstated qualities

By gregandcharlie on October 26, 2016Verified Purchase

The write-up makes a point to herald the products "solid wood" which might lead one to believe that it is something other than what you get when you open the boxes. For the most part there are very, very few solid wood pieces and plenty of thin veneer over press board. Doors, drawers and panels are thin making the working parts of the cart not quite the quality one would hope. The wheels are OK but not as heavy duty as they could be. I am going to build an inset black base and go with a stationary piece. The one exception of quality is the granite top which is very substantial. The instructions were very well written and easy to follow. Took two of us about an hour to put it together. Chose not to use the towel and spice racks as I was looking for a cleaner look.
