Great game table!

By Myra Lehmann on December 18, 2016Verified Purchase

I bought this table so I could play board games with my grandchildren in the living room. It arrived promptly and in perfect condition. It is good quality and was reasonably priced. The only disappointment I have is that the wheels don't move both directions, only back and forth in one direction. Otherwise I absolutely love it. The color of the wood is much nicer than the picture shows, much less yellow. It is solid wood that resembles driftwood. The bottom rack is very convenient for keeping the top cleared off when playing games, and there is plenty of room for more players when appropriate..
