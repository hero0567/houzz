Product Description

This simply crafted and skillfully structured table made out of robust iron and the foldable feet is a must due to its manifold utility and uncomplicated style. The top of the table is covered with an immaculate glass sheet whereas the rest of the body and the feet are constructed with iron that makes it durable. The table can be used during tea time or it can be placed in the lawn where you could sit and enjoy an early morning breakfast or a Sunday brunch. The body of the table has been refined with metal finish which ensures longevity and prevent from rusting. The round metal frame of the table has been designed to avoided bruises and sharp edges. Due its foldable feet and lightweight body, it's totally effortless to carry along during outings. At home, it can be conveniently kept in a small corner when not in use.
