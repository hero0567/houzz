Product Description

The contemporary Yohkoh Barstool by Pastel Furniture has a simple yet elegant design that is perfect for any decor. An ideal way to add a touch of modern flair to any dining or entertaining area in your home. This barstool features a quality frame with sturdy legs and foot rest finished in Chrome and Walnut Veneer. The padded seat is upholstered in Up Ivory or Up Black offering comfort and style. Pastel Furniture manufactures superior furniture with a distinguished elegance. They have created an innovative line of swivel bar stools and counter stools that fit into rooms of all styles.
